Common Mental Health Conditions

Understanding these conditions is an important first step. Each card provides an overview and links to more detailed information.

Anxiety Disorders

When worry becomes constant and affects daily life. Includes generalized anxiety, social anxiety, and panic disorder.

Learn More →

Depression

More than sadness — it affects energy, sleep, appetite, and motivation. Major depression and persistent depressive disorder.

Learn More →

ADHD

Attention challenges, impulse control, and activity levels that affect work, school, and relationships.

Learn More →

OCD

Obsessive-compulsive disorder involves unwanted thoughts and repetitive behaviors that interfere with daily life.

Learn More →

PTSD

Post-traumatic stress disorder develops after experiencing or witnessing traumatic events.

Learn More →

Eating Disorders

Includes anorexia, bulimia, and binge eating disorder. Serious conditions requiring professional support.

Learn More →

Bipolar Disorder

Characterized by extreme mood swings — from depressive lows to manic highs. Manageable with proper support.

Learn More →

Substance Use Disorder

Addiction and substance abuse affect mental health and physical wellbeing. Recovery is possible with support.

Learn More →

Co-occurring Disorders

Multiple mental health conditions that often occur together. Integrated treatment addresses all conditions.

Learn More →
