Who We Are

Meet the Founders

We created this platform because we believe mental health education should be accessible, compassionate, and stigma-free. Our goal is to empower students, families, and communities by providing reliable information and a safe space for learning.

Dhairya

Dhairya is one of the co-founders of this platform. After seeing classmates, friends, and community members silently struggle with anxiety, depression, and stress, he became passionate about creating a space where all people could access helpful, trustworthy information. He believes awareness is the first step toward healing.

Rishi

Rishi is a co-founder committed to spreading mental-health understanding across schools and youth communities. After noticing how many people felt alone in their experiences, he wanted to help build a platform that offers clarity, education, and support. His goal is to make mental-health resources accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We started this website because we have seen firsthand how many people, especially teenagers, struggle quietly with mental-health challenges. Many feel misunderstood, judged, or unsure where to turn. We created this platform to spread awareness, reduce stigma, and provide reliable, compassionate information that can help people understand themselves and others better.

Together, we believe in building a community where mental health is talked about openly, where people feel supported, and where every individual knows: You Matter.
